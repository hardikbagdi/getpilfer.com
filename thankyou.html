Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I grant access to the app?

We need to save your photos to your Google account. The app reads the image you want to save and writes it into Google servers. We ask only for access to you basic profile info and managing your photos.
We don't track any activity you perform at all.

Do I've to install any app on my smartphone?

The only app you need on your phone is Google Photos App using which you can access photos you save. If you've enabled auto sync feature on the app, then photos saved using Pilfer will be synced to your phone instantly.
